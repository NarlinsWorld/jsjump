  1. <link rel="stylesheet" href="https://maxcdn.bootstrapcdn.com/bootstrap/4.5.2/css/bootstrap.min.css">
  2. <script src="https://ajax.googleapis.com/ajax/libs/jquery/3.5.1/jquery.min.js"></script>
  3. <script src="https://cdnjs.cloudflare.com/ajax/libs/popper.js/1.16.0/umd/popper.min.js"></script>
  4. <script src="https://maxcdn.bootstrapcdn.com/bootstrap/4.5.2/js/bootstrap.min.js"></script>

Run a Match Race: 2 Boats

1) Set the name or owner for each boat. (Any time even during race)
2) Set the dp-n for each boat.
3) Real time is always displayed at the top of the page.
4) Start a race by pressing START.
5) Upon pressing START:
  a) the race start time will be displayed
  b) Elapsed Time is just under the starting time
  c) corrected time (CTA and CTB) for both boats is diplayed.
6) When the 1st boat (either A or B) gets to the 1st mark, press Btn for that split and boat.
  a) Elapsed Time at the moment of Btn press is displayed.
  b) Corrected Time at the moment of Btn press is displayed.
7) When the 2nd boat gets to the same mark, press Btn for that boat.
8) When a split has been pressed for both boats, the difference |A-B| and boat in front will appear.
9) All splits work the same. The finish is just another split with a different label.
Before running a 2nd race, push the RESET button at the bottom of the page.
